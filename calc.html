What is calculus?

In a nutshell, calculus is the study of the rate of change of a "system". A system is anything that we are conducting our studies on. It can be dynamic (that is, changing) or static. Regardless of what we are studying, if we find ourselves working with rates of change, it can be identified as a calculus problem. However, calculus doesn't just stop there, we can use it to describe the motion of almost anything, to find the area or volume of any geometric shape, or to find the amount of interest we can get from a continuously compounding asset.
